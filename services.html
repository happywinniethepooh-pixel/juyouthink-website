Our Approach我们的方法论

Research Built for Decisions为决策而生的研究

Structured yet flexible methodology ensuring every project delivers clarity, not just data.结构化而灵活的方法论，确保每个项目交付的是清晰洞察，而不仅仅是数据。

1

Co-Creation Brief共创简报

Cross-functional workshops to align on the real business question behind the research request.以跨职能工作坊形式，在研究设计初期就对齐企业真正的业务问题。

2

Custom Design定制设计

Select and blend the right mix of qualitative, quantitative, and digital methods for your objectives.选择并融合定性、定量、数字方法的最佳组合，量身定制研究方案。

3

Senior-Led Fieldwork资深主导执行

Project leads personally conduct or supervise all interviews — no outsourcing of quality.项目负责人亲自带队或全程监督所有访谈——绝不让质量假手于人。

4

Actionable Output可落地成果

Reports go beyond findings — prioritized recommendations and internal activation sessions.报告不只呈现发现——更包含优先级建议与内部激活工作坊。

🏠

In-Home Depth Interviews入户深访

Visit consumers in their own space for authentic, uninhibited conversations. Observe real product usage and home environments that reveal insights no survey can capture.走进用户家中，在熟悉的环境中展开真实、无拘束的对话。观察真实产品使用场景和家居环境，挖掘问卷无法捕捉的深层洞察。

💬

Focus Group Discussions小组座谈会

Bring together diverse consumer profiles to explore category dynamics, validate hypotheses, and surface group norms that shape purchasing decisions.汇聚不同背景的消费者群体，探索品类动态，验证研究假设，挖掘影响购买决策的群体共识。

📱

Online Quantitative Survey在线定量调研

Validate qualitative discoveries at scale with statistically robust surveys of 1,000+ samples to quantify attitudes, preferences, and market sizing.以千人级统计稳健的在线问卷验证定性发现，量化态度、偏好并估算市场规模。
